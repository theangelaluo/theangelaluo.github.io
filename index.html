About Me

Angela Luo

I am a junior at Stanford University majoring in Symbolic Systems with a concentration in Human-Computer Interaction, and minoring in Economics. As a well-rounded full-stack engineer with strong communication and leadership skills, I enjoy working with others on high-impact problems at the edge of technology. Currently, I am seeking internship opportunities in software engineering and related fields for the summer of 2019.

I grew up in the Bay Area and was a competitive figure skater for 12 years and a dancer for 14. An artist and daydreamer at heart, I have also published poetry in multiple print and online journals. Being involved in my community is very important to me - whether it is teaching figure skating to special needs kids on the weekends, mentoring high school students in their school subjects and college applications, serving food to low-income families in the Tenderloin every holiday season, or being a student staff member in my residential dorm, I strive to make an impact on the lives of people around me.

You can reach me at: theangelaluo@gmail.com
